About HNZ School

A Legacy of
Inspired Learning

HNZ School stands as one of Ramanagara's most distinguished institutions for primary education. We believe every child carries within them an extraordinary potential — and it is our privilege to help them discover it.

From the first day of Grade 1 to the final steps of Grade 5, students at HNZ are surrounded by dedicated educators, enriching activities, and a culture that prizes both academic excellence and strong character.

"The inspiration of the excellence."

— HNZ School, Ramanagara
